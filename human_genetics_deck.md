## Human Genetics-Specific Game Modes

### Game 13: Genetic Blackjack

**Players:** 2-4  
**Time:** 20-30 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Hit target phenotype score

**Setup:**
- Choose a phenotype target (Energy, Social, Health, etc.)
- Target score: 21
- Deal 2 cards face down to each player

**Turn:**
1. Look at your cards
2. Sum effect values for chosen phenotype
3. Choose: Hit (draw) or Stand

**Scoring:**
- Closest to 21 without exceeding wins
- Exact 21 with 2 cards = "Genetic Jackpot" (2× payout)
- Bust (>21) = Immediate loss

**Variant - Multi-trait:**
Balance 3 phenotypes simultaneously. Must not exceed 15 in any single phenotype while maximizing total.

---

### Game 14: Ancestry Journey

**Players:** 2-6  
**Time:** 40-50 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Map traits to world regions

**Setup:**
- Deal 7 cards each
- Place world map board in centre with region zones
- Each region starts empty

**Turn:**
1. Play 1-2 cards to regions on the map
2. Card must have that region as Peak or Low
3. First card in a region establishes it
4. Subsequent cards must share at least one trait type

**Scoring:**
| Achievement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Connected path (3+ adjacent regions) | +20 |
| Most diverse (cards in 5+ regions) | +25 |
| Regional dominance (4+ cards in one region) | +15 |
| Transcontinental (cards on 3+ continents) | +30 |
| First to empty hand | +15 |

**Migration Bonus:** If you play a card that "bridges" two continents (has Peak in one, Low in another), score +10.

### Game 15: Natural Selection Survival

**Players:** 4-7  
**Time:** 30-40 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Survive environmental challenges

**Setup:**
- Deal 5 cards each (your "genome")
- Shuffle environment deck separately

**Environment Cards:**
| Environment | Favoured Traits | Penalized Traits |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Famine | +Thrifty metabolism | -Lean metabolism |
| Plague | +HIV resist, +Malaria resist | -Autoimmune |
| Migration North | +Lactase, +Vitamin D efficient | -Melanin |
| UV Exposure | +Melanin | -Fair skin, -Red hair |
| Cold Climate | +Thrifty, +Dense bones | -Thin build |
| Altitude | +High VO2max | -Asthma risk |

**Round:**
1. Flip environment card
2. Calculate survival score:
   - Favoured trait: +3 per card
   - Penalized trait: -3 per card
   - Neutral: +0
3. Players below 0 total lose one card (their choice)
4. Players at 0 cards are eliminated

**Winning:** Last player with cards OR most cards when environment deck exhausts.

### Game 16: Genetic Counsellor (Deduction)

**Players:** 3-5  
**Time:** 25-35 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Deduce opponent's hidden genome

**Setup:**
- Each player draws 5 cards secretly
- These represent their hidden "genome"
- Take notes as you gather information

**Turn:**
1. Ask ONE yes/no question to ONE opponent:
   - "Do you have any Sensory cards?"
   - "Do you have a frequency above 30%?"
   - "Do you have any European peak cards?"
   - "Do you have h² above 0.8?"
2. Opponent must answer truthfully
3. Make a guess (optional): Name all 5 cards

**Scoring:**
| Achievement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Correct guess (all 5 cards) | 25 |
| Partial guess (3-4 correct) | 10 |
| Wrong guess | -5 |
| Survive to end unguessed | 15 |

**Educational Value:** Teaches inheritance patterns, frequency distributions, and regional genetics.

## Educational Notes

### Data Sources
All genetic data derived from:
- gnomAD (Genome Aggregation Database)
- 1000 Genomes Project
- Published GWAS studies
- Twin study heritability estimates

### Scientific Caveats
- Single variants rarely determine traits absolutely
- Environment significantly modifies expression
- Population frequencies are averages with high individual variation
- Heritability is population-specific, not individual

### Ethical Considerations
This game simplifies complex genetic concepts for educational entertainment. Genetic determinism should be avoided; traits emerge from gene-environment interactions.
