# Cambrian Prehistory Traits Deck

## Overview
80 cards covering evolutionary innovations from the origin of life through the Permian extinction (3,500-252 MYA). Focus on the foundational traits that built animal body plans.

## Cambrian-Specific Game Modes

### Game 9: Extinction Survivor

**Players:** 4-7  
**Time:** 30-40 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Survival through extinction events

**Setup:**
- Deal 5 cards each
- Shuffle extinction event cards into bottom third of deck

**Extinction Events:**
| Event | MYA | Effect |
|-------|-----|--------|
| End-Ordovician | 444 | Traits with MYA > 444 lose 1 life |
| Late Devonian | 372 | Traits with MYA > 372 lose 1 life |
| End-Permian | 252 | All traits lose 1 life |

**Gameplay:**
- Each card starts with 2 lives (use tokens)
- When extinction flips, apply damage
- Cards at 0 lives are discarded
- Protected cards (in melds) lose lives last

**Winning:** Last player with cards OR highest total MYA when deck empties.

**Strategy:** Ancient traits survive better but score lower; recent traits are powerful but risky.

---

### Game 10: Ecosystem Builder

**Players:** 2-4  
**Time:** 45-60 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Food web construction

**Setup:**
- Deal 8 cards each
- Place "Filter Feeding" card as ecosystem base

**Building Rules:**
- Predators must connect to prey (use Enables/Prereq)
- Feeding cards connect to Locomotion (catching prey)
- Defence cards block connections (protection)
- Respiration cards are required for active organisms

**Food Web Levels:**
| Level | Requirements | Points |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| Primary Producer | Filter feeding, Respiration | 5 |
| Herbivore | Feeding, Locomotion | 10 |
| Predator | Jaws or Proboscis, Locomotion | 15 |
| Apex | Venom or Size advantage | 25 |

**Scoring:**
- Complete food chain (4+ levels): +30
- Balanced ecosystem (all suits): +20
- Most species (cards played): +15

---

### Game 11: Cambrian Explosion

**Players:** 3-6  
**Time:** 25-35 min  
**Key Mechanic:** Race to diversify body plans

**Setup:**
- Shuffle deck, deal 5 each
- All players start at "Bilateral Symmetry" (shared ancestor)

**Turn:**
1. Play a card branching from existing tree
2. Must share at least one clade marker with parent
3. MYA must be equal or later than parent
4. Draw a card

**Diversification Bonuses:**
| Achievement | Bonus |
|-------------|-------|
| First to play 3 different clades | +15 |
| First complete suit set | +20 |
| Longest single branch | +10 |
| Most body plans (unique prereqs) | +25 |

**Explosion Round:**
- When someone plays their 8th card, trigger Explosion
- All players get 2 more turns
- Most diverse phylogeny wins

## Educational Notes

### Key Evolutionary Transitions
Cards are designed to teach major transitions:
1. Prokaryote → Eukaryote (endosymbiosis)
2. Single cell → Multicellular (division of labour)
3. Radial → Bilateral (directional movement)
4. Water → Land (lungs, limbs, amniotic egg)

### Cambrian Explosion Focus
25 cards from 538-485 MYA represent the explosion of body plans that established all modern animal phyla in approximately 20 million years.

### Extinction Events
The three special extinction cards represent real mass extinctions that shaped evolutionary history:
- End-Ordovician (444 MYA): 85% species lost
- Late Devonian (372 MYA): 75% species lost
- End-Permian (252 MYA): 96% species lost ("The Great Dying")
